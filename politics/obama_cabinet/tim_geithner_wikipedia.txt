'''Timothy Franz Geithner''' (pronounced /ˈɡaɪtnər/; born August 18, 1961), was the 75th United States Secretary of the Treasury from 2009 to 2013. He served under President of the Untied StatesPresident Barack Obama. Before becoming Secretary of the Treasury, he was  president of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York CityNew York.
