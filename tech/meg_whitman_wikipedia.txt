'''Margaret Cushing Whitman''' (born August 4, 1956) is an American billionaire.cite newsurl=http://www.forbes.com/lists/2010/10/billionaires-2010_Margaret-Whitman_5AW7.html title=#773 Margaret Whitman work=Forbesdate=February 12, 2010 accessdate=August 29, 2010  She is the president and chief executive officer of Hewlett-Packard.cite press releasetitle=HP Names Meg Whitman President and Chief Executive Officerurl=http://www.hp.com/hpinfo/newsroom/press/2011/110922xb.html?mtxs=rss-corp-newsdate=September 22, 2011publisher=Hewlett-Packardaccessdate=September 22, 2011  

She was CEO of the online auction site eBay from 1998 to 2008.  She also worked for DreamWorks, Procter & Gamble, and Hasbro. In 2010, she ran for Governor of California as a United States Republican PartyRepublican.  Her campaign was paid for mostly by millions of her own money.  Even though she spent over $100 million, she still lost to Jerry Brown.cite news first=Philip last=Caulfield title=Meg Whitman loses California governor race despite $160&nbsp;million tab; Jerry Brown wins for 3rd time url=http://www.nydailynews.com/news/politics/2010/11/03/2010-11-03_meg_whitman_loses_california_governor_race_despite_140_million_tab_jerry_brown_w.html publisher=Daily News (New York)Daily News date=November 3, 2010 accessdate=November 4, 2010
