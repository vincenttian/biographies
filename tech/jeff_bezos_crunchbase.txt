Overview:
Jeffrey Preston Bezos, originally of Albuquerque, New Mexico,  is the Founder, President, Chief Executive Officer &amp; Chairman of the board of Amazon.com. 

Bezos graduated from Princeton University Phi Beta Kappa. Prior to founding Amazon in 1994, he worked as a Financial Analyst for D. E. Shaw &amp; Co. 

Time magazine named Bezos the Person of the Year in 1999.
